BELONGING

1. Close or intimate relationship; mutual loyalty.

2. [Belong] To become attached or bound (as to a person, group, or organization) by birth, allegiance, or dependency.

Prabal Gurung’s spring/summer 2020 collection, which celebrated his eponymous label’s tenth anniversary, combined elements of American sportswear with features of traditional Eastern dress. Here, the triangular cutout at the waist—a signature of Gurung’s work—references the wrapped construction of an Indian sari. The statement on the sash, "Who Gets to Be American?," reflects the designer’s activism on behalf of immigrants as well as his own path to becoming an American citizen.
